Opera Cakes

Opera cake (French: Gâteau opéra) is a French cake. It is made with layers of almond sponge cake (known as Joconde in French) soaked in coffee syrup, layered with ganache and coffee (or Grand Marnier) French buttercream, and covered in a chocolate glaze.

Some believe this cake got its name as the layers resemble the levels of an opera theatre.

Mousse Cakes

Mousses are almost always cold dishes, sweet mousses sometimes being served frozen. They may be stabilized by the addition of gelatin. Chocolate and mocha mousses are sometimes made with a custard base. For a fruit mousse, pureed fruit or juice replaces the milk in the custard.

Mousse is pronounced “moose.” It is in no way associated with the animal.
